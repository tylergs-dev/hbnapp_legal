Hushabye App

Welcome to the Hushabye app support page. We’re here to help you get the most out of your experience.

About the app

The Hushabye app provides easy access to course materials, upcoming events, and helpful reminders—all in one place. Designed to support your journey, the app allows you to stay connected and informed anytime.

Frequently asked questions

How do I access the course content?

Open the app and navigate to the Learn section to view all available materials.

How do event reminders work?

You can view upcoming events in the calendar and enable notifications to receive reminders of all events.

I’m not receiving notifications—what should I do?

Make sure notifications are enabled for the app in your device settings.

The app isn’t working properly—how can I fix it?

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue continues, restarting your device may help. If neither work you can reach out to our support email.

Contact us

You can email us directly at apps@hushabyenursery.org, or use the form below. Submitting the form will open your email app with your message ready to send.
